Who We Are

A Place of Power, Praise & Purpose

Champions Nation Christian Centre (CNCC) is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community where the Word of God is boldly proclaimed and lives are radically changed.

We believe in raising champions — men and women who walk in the fullness of their God-given destiny, equipped to impact their world for the Kingdom.
